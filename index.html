AI PING PONG GAME

Instructions

MOVEMENT :
Move cursor up and down to move your paddle
COLOUR :
Your Paddle Colour: Red
Computer Paddle Colour: Orange
RULES :
Speed gathers by 0.5 every time the ball is hit (by computer or you)
If computer gets 4 points it's game over
THE TRUTH :
You can't win .-.
CHALLENGE:
See if you can get Speed 20 :)
Press "space" or the X icon to go back to the game
